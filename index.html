Module 04

Yoga & Mobility Protocol

Developer posture causes stiff hips and a tight chest. Supple muscles look athletic, blocky muscles look stiff. This also acts as a primary gut-health intervention.

AM Gut-Flow (5 Minutes)

Perform immediately upon waking, after warm water. Massages digestive organs to release gas prior to working out.

1. Pawanmuktasana (Wind-Relieving)

Lie on back, hug knees to chest, lift head to knees. Hold 60s, breathe deep into belly.

2. Malasana (Yogi Squat)

Deep squat, feet flat, elbows push knees apart. Hold 60s. Fantastic for bowel movement.

3. Cat-Cow Stretch

On hands and knees, arch and round back. 10 slow reps synchronised with breath.

4. Child's Pose

Rest hips on heels, stretch arms forward. 60 seconds deep breathing.

PM Desk-Relief (10 Minutes)

Crucial for undoing 8+ hours of screen-time posture. Do this before bed.

  • Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana)

    2x 30 seconds. Opens tight developer chest and rounds out the shoulders.

  • Kneeling Lunge Stretch

    60 seconds per leg. Opens hip flexors shortened by sitting in a chair all day.

  • Doorway Pectoral Stretch

    60 seconds per arm. Direct fix for internally rotated "laptop posture".

Module 05

Cardio & Skipping Protocol

While bulking, cardio is NOT for burning calories. It is strictly for cardiovascular stamina, calf development, and athletic agility. Keep it short and explosive.

When to Execute

Attach to the end of Friday's Lower Body session, OR do it on Wednesday (Recovery day). Max 2x per week.

Skipping Progression

L1
The Base (Weeks 1-4)

Basic two-foot bounce. Wrist rotation, no arm swings. Keep jumps 1 inch high.

5 Rounds: 60s jump / 30s rest
L2
The Boxer (Weeks 5-8)

Boxer skip (shifting weight side to side) and high knees.

8 Rounds: 45s jump / 15s rest
L3
Gladiator Engine (Weeks 9+)

Double unders integration. Maximum speed bursts.

Tabata: 8 Rounds of 20s MAX speed / 10s rest

Module 08

Gut & Bloat Optimization

Jain diets rely heavily on legumes (phytic acid). Combined with sedentary coding, this causes severe morning gas. Here is the operational fix.

System Conflicts

  • Eating heavy legumes (rajma) at night. They ferment while sleeping.
  • Insufficient soaking of beans/dals before cooking.
  • Eating raw oats or sprouts (extremely hard to digest).
  • Heavy meals before 6 AM training routing blood away from gut.

Operational Fixes

  • Rule of Night: Only light proteins (Moong dal, milk) at dinner.
  • 12-Hour Soak: Soak beans overnight. Boil in fresh water.
  • Spice Layering: Add Hing and Ajwain to the tadka of every dal.
  • AM Protocol: Warm water + fennel upon waking. Wait 15m. Bathroom.
Jain-Safe Probiotics

No pills needed. Eat 1 small bowl of fresh homemade curd with lunch daily. Drink Buttermilk (chaash) with roasted cumin mid-afternoon.

Bi-Weekly Decision Matrix

Analyze your Sunday weigh-ins every 14 days.

  • Weight same + Strength up: Recomposition happening. Hold calories.
  • Weight down: Burning too much. Add 1 roti + 1 glass milk.
  • Weight up > 1kg: Dirty bulk alert. Gaining fat. Cut snacks by half.
  • Weight up 0.25-0.5kg: PERFECT VELOCITY. Change nothing.
Module 13

Mindset & Fatal Errors

The physical mechanics are simple. The psychological execution is where 95% of people fail. Read this weekly to stay calibrated.

The "Dirty Bulk" Trap

"I'm bulking so I can eat junk." No. You are 64kg skinny-fat. If you eat sugar and junk, you will just get a belly and a soft face. Stay strict.

Program Hopping

Doing this routine for 3 weeks, watching a YouTube video, and switching. It takes 8-12 weeks for a program to show results. Stick to the system.

Ego Lifting

Sacrificing form to lift heavier dumbbells. The muscle doesn't know the number on the weight; it only knows tension. Control the downward phase.

Ignoring Gut Health

If you are bloated, you cannot absorb nutrients properly. Digestion > Consumption. Follow the Jain soaking and spice rules religiously.

The Spartan Code

"You do not rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems."

As a developer, treat your body like a codebase. Consistency is your version control. A 70% perfect workout done consistently for a year destroys a 100% perfect workout done inconsistently. Show up, execute the code, close the laptop, and rest.
