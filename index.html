Target Architecture

At 64 kg and 175 cm, you're carrying maybe 52–54 kg of lean mass. For the "Greek god" look at your height, you need roughly 58–60 kg of lean mass at 12–14% body fat, putting your target at 67–70 kg. The 70 kg target is realistic and lands you firmly in the aesthetic zone without looking bulky.

The 6 kg added must be muscle. This requires 3 inputs: consistent progressive overload, caloric surplus with adequate protein, and deep sleep. Miss any one, and the timeline doubles.

Module 02

Hybrid Training Split

Push/Pull/Legs split, 6 days per week, with one active rest day. Gives each muscle group 2x frequency per week — optimal for hypertrophy in beginners.

Progressive Overload Rules
  • Add 1.25–2.5 kg to compound barbell lifts every 1–2 weeks.
  • Add 1 rep per set before adding weight on dumbbell lifts.
  • When you can complete all prescribed sets at the top of the rep range, increase weight next session.
  • Log every set in the Action Center. If you're not tracking, you're guessing.
  • Session Target: Warmup (5m) + Main (45-55m) + Cooldown (5m) = 60 mins.

While bulking, cardio is NOT for burning calories. It is strictly for cardiovascular stamina, calf development, and athletic agility. Skip on 3 days per week (Tue, Thu, Sat).

Weeks 1-4		 Warmup: 2m bounce

Phase 2: Endurance
Weeks 5-8		 Warmup: 2m bounce
Intervals: 45s skip / 20s rest × 10		 7.5m active / ~12m total Add alternate feet (running step) every other round.
Phase 3: Athletic
Weeks 9-12		 B1: 60s speed / 15s rest × 4

Module 08

Gut & Bloat Optimization

Jain diets rely heavily on legumes (phytic acid). Combined with sedentary coding, this causes severe morning gas. Here is the operational fix.

Root Causes

  • Workout proximity: Exercising on partially digested meal routes blood from gut.
  • Legume oligosaccharides: Unsoaked dals/soy ferment in the colon.
  • Desk posture: Compresses abdominal organs, slows gastric motility.
  • Irregular timing: Disrupts migrating motor complex (MMC).

Operational Fixes

  • 12-Hour Soak: Soak all beans overnight. Boil with Hing + Ginger + Turmeric.
  • Timing: 60m pre-workout gap. Dinner by 8:30 PM. 10-12hr overnight fast.
  • Water separation: Drink between meals, not during (small sips only).
  • Probiotics: 100-200g fresh homemade curd daily. Buttermilk at lunch.

Module 09

Recovery & Sleep Protocol

Muscle is not built in the gym; it is torn there. It is built in bed. As a developer, your nervous system is constantly stimulated by screens. We must down-regulate it.

Sleep Targets

Total: 7.5–8 hrs. Muscle growth (GH secretion) peaks in deep sleep.

Bedtime: 10:30 PM consistently. Wake: 6:00 AM.

Latency: < 15 min to fall asleep. If longer, you're overstimulated.

Pre-Sleep Routine

  • 10:00 PM Screens off. Blue light blocks melatonin.
  • 10:05 PM Golden milk. Sip slowly.
  • 10:10 PM 5m Legs-Up-The-Wall (parasympathetic).
  • 10:15 PM 4-7-8 Breathing (4 cycles).
  • 10:25 PM Lights out. Room 22-24°C.

Muscle Soreness Management

Level Action
Mild (can train) Train as planned. Warm up extra 3 min.
Moderate (hurts to move) Train different muscle group. Light stretch sore area.
Severe (limits tasks) Rest. Light yoga only. Extra protein. Sleep more.
Persists > 5 days Possible injury, not DOMS. Rest + assess.

Module 13

Mindset & Fatal Errors

The physical mechanics are simple. The psychological execution is where 95% of people fail. Read this weekly to stay calibrated.

1. The "Dirty Bulk" Trap

"I'm bulking so I can eat junk." No. You are 64kg skinny-fat. Junk adds a belly and a soft face. Stay strict.

2. Skipping Meals for Code

You cannot build muscle in a deficit. Carry pre-prepped snacks. The compiler can wait 5 minutes.

3. Training Without a Log

If you're not writing down weights and reps, you're not progressively overloading — you're just exercising.

4. Ego Lifting

At 64 kg, your bench is 30–40 kg. Perfect form at low weight builds more muscle than garbage form at high weight.

5. Ignoring Legs

"Greek god" requires proportionate quads, hams, and calves. Chicken legs ruin the entire aesthetic.

6. Relying on Dal for Protein

Dal gives ~9g pro/cup. You'd need 18+ cups. Soya and paneer MUST be daily staples.

7. Sacrificing Sleep

Muscles grow during sleep. Sacrificing sleep for a PR means sacrificing gains. The body can't wait.

8. Dehydration

If you're thirsty, you're already dehydrated. Keep a bottle at the desk. 3L minimum.

9. Impatience

First 4-6 weeks are neurological. Visible changes start month 2-3. Trust the trajectory.

10. Overcomplicating

Eat protein, lift heavier than last week, sleep 8h. That's 90%. Stop researching, start executing.

The Spartan Code

"You do not rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems."

As a developer, treat your body like a codebase. Consistency is your version control. A 70% perfect workout done consistently for a year destroys a 100% perfect workout done inconsistently. Show up, execute the code, close the laptop, and rest.

Gujarat Climate Protocol

Ahmedabad demands environmental periodization. Heat, humidity, and sun exposure alter your baseline requirements.

Gujarat Vitamin D Protocol

The Advantage

Ahmedabad latitude (~23°N) provides strong UVB year-round. 15 mins is enough.

The Rules

10:00 AM - 12:00 PM. Expose arms and face. NO sunscreen during this 15m window.

Post-Sun

Wait 30 mins before showering (synthesis continues on skin).

Season Adjustments
Summer
(Mar–Jun | 42–46°C)
  • Hydration: 4+ litres. Add 500ml coconut water + 500ml buttermilk.
  • Coolants: Lemon + rock salt + jaggery water. Increase cucumber, lauki, mint. Decrease heavy ghee.
  • Skin: Switch to lighter sesame/sunflower oil for body. Sandalwood + rose water face mask.
  • Training: Shift to 6 AM or 7 PM. NEVER train 10 AM - 5 PM.
Winter
(Nov–Feb | 10–25°C)
  • Fats: +1 tsp ghee per meal. Fat-soluble vitamins absorb better.
  • Heat: Hot soups, khichdi. Increase ginger and black pepper.
  • Skin/Hair: Sesame oil for body (heavier). Warm hair oil more. Double face moisture.
  • Immunity: Tulsi tea (2-3 leaves in hot water) + amla.
Monsoon
(Jul–Oct | 70–90% Hum)
  • Gut Defense: Cooked greens ONLY (no raw salads). Boil/filter water. Increase ajwain/hing.
  • Safety: Avoid outside pre-cut fruit. Cook sprouts. No leftovers.
  • Skin: Reduce oil moisturizer. Use aloe gel. Multani mitti 2x/week to control oil.
  • Vit D: UVB penetrates light clouds. Still go outside daily.
